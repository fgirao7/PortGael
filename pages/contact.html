Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the recruitment process take?

For Healthcare Assistants, the process usually takes between 1 and 4 weeks. For Nurses, it can take up to 8 weeks, depending on NMBI registration status and employer requirements.

Do I need to be registered with NMBI?

For nursing roles, yes. We can assist with your NMBI application and guide you through the registration process.

What level of English is required?

You need to be able to communicate clearly in English with residents, families, and the care team. Some positions may have higher requirements.

Do you charge candidates for your services?

No. Our services are completely free for candidates. Employers pay our recruitment fees.
